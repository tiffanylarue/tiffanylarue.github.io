### Belay Buddy Web App
Belay Buddy is an online community for climbers, allowing them to create events, find belay partners and gyms, share climbing equipment, and expand their community. Developers include Mitchell Avery, Chris Taylor, Tiffany LaRue, and Eric McAfee. Demo by Tiffany LaRue. Completed April 2025.

### Text Summarization Using NLP
Continuous learning is more important today than ever to keep up with the pace of knowledge and for many jobs. People want to be able to digest high-quality textbook content, but have competing priorities limiting their time. My project addresses this issue by testing the quality of various text summarization methods on text passages from the AI and Systems textbooks used in CSPB. The methods tested include extractive summarizartion, such as SUMY’s LSA Summarizer, and it also includes abstractive summarization using transformer models such as BART and PEGASUS. Evaluation will be done with ROUGE, BERTScore, and human evaluation. Author: Tiffany LaRue. Completed April 2026.
